## MDX Components

### Callouts

<Callout type="note">
This is a **note** callout. Use it for general annotations or asides that don't fit neatly into the main text.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
This is an **info** callout. Use it to highlight background context or supplementary information.
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip">
This is a **tip** callout. Use it for practical advice or recommendations.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
This is a **warning** callout. Use it for caveats, gotchas, or things the reader should be cautious about.
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip" title="Custom title">
Callouts accept an optional `title` prop that overrides the default label.
</Callout>

### Pull quote

<PullQuote>
Good typography is invisible — the reader focuses on content, not on the mechanics of reading.
</PullQuote>

<PullQuote attribution="Robert Bringhurst">
Typography exists to honor content.

### Aside

The aside component renders as an inline block on narrow screens and floats into the right margin on wide screens.

<Aside label="Note">
This is a margin note. On wide viewports it sits beside the paragraph that precedes it, keeping the main column uninterrupted.
</Aside>

An aside without a label works too — it's just a quieter annotation in the margin.

<Aside>
A label-less aside for smaller observations.
</Aside>
